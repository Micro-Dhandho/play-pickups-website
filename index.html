Frequently Asked Questions

How does Play Pickups work?

Once players register on the website, the platform allows anybody to organise or join a game. Players can pick locations, format of play and even minimum skill level.

Once all players are finalized for a pickup game, everyone is sent a message with the details of the location, contact details of the other player and any other important details.

Do you have a mobile app?

Not at the moment. We will launch one in the near future.

What if there are not enough players in my city?

We will be rolling out to more and more cities based on a minimum number of players required to make the platform more useful to everyone. If you'd like us to roll out in your city, share this with your friends and get more people to sign up!
